Solvec-AI

A systematic futures research desk.

Solvec-AI is an AI-powered systematic trading venture I co-founded. We built a full research platform on top of a world-class, ~161 GB Level-3 (order-by-order) data archive, then paired it with a disciplined, pre-registered validation process. The goal is simple to state and hard to earn: find trade setups that genuinely survive honest testing, and refuse to claim an edge that hasn't.

As co-founder, I work on the trading side of the desk, shaping the strategy logic and the live execution view that the systematic research is built to validate, alongside a builder who engineers the platform and a fellow trader.

01

One desk, many modules

The platform is organized as a single research desk with dedicated modules for data, strategy, validation, crash analysis, a trade library, a live terminal, and backtesting. Everything reports to one honest state: what works, what is next, and what has been ruled out.

02

Validation over hype

The core philosophy is intellectual honesty. Roughly 161 GB of raw Level-3 order flow feeds a process that has forward-walked three strategy candidates, killed eight hypotheses on a pre-registered gate, and studied 157 real trades in a tape study. No result is dressed up: if a candidate has not cleared a clean, forward, out-of-sample test, we say so plainly.

03

Kill on the gate

Every hypothesis is tested against pre-registered statistical gates before it is allowed to survive. Candidates that clear significance and bootstrap testing but fail the strict multiple-testing gate are held back, not promoted. What has survived is an architecture rather than a single trigger: a regime-aware, cross-asset, risk-first framework. The exact mechanics stay in-house.

04

Anatomy of a liquidity crash

Because we own full order-by-order data, we can reconstruct exactly what happened inside a real market event. This teardown of the April 2025 tariff crash rebuilds the S&P (E-mini) order book event by event, exposing what bar charts and standard depth data simply cannot see: true signed order flow, resting-depth evaporation, hidden iceberg refills, and the difference between real aggression and quote noise.

05

The dealer-positioning terminal

The terminal is a real-time cockpit for institutional options positioning: net gamma exposure, vanna and charm, implied-volatility skew, dark-pool prints, and live options flow, all in one view. It is used as a regime and context gate, never as a standalone trade trigger.

06

Backtest and replay

A dedicated backtest registry and replay terminal lets us plug a strategy in and test it against the historical Level-3 data we own, replaying order flow and volume profile trade by trade. Strict guardrails keep everything research-and-paper only: no live capital sits behind any of it yet.

Solvec-AI is in active development, and we are documenting the build publicly, milestone by milestone.
